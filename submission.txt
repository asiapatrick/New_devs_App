Additional Note (not covered in Loom video due to time):

Bug 3 - Frontend Rounding Precision (RevenueSummary.tsx)

After recording the Loom, I identified a third bug causing the finance team's "few cents off" issue.

Root cause: In frontend/src/components/RevenueSummary.tsx (line 64), the total revenue was being rounded to 2 decimal places using Math.round(data.total_revenue * 100) / 100 before display. Since the database stores total_amount as NUMERIC(10, 3), any value with a third decimal (e.g. $1234.567) would be displayed as $1234.57 — dropping a fraction of a cent.

Fix: Removed the Math.round call and updated maximumFractionDigits from 2 to 3 so the displayed value matches the stored precision exactly.
